About Us – Trusted Bee Products & Beeswax Candle Supplier Since 2004

HANGZHOU AADON TRADING CO., LTD. has been specializing in the international trade of bee products and candles since its establishment in 2004. The company is primarily engaged in the export of natural bee products such as HONEY, ROYAL JELLY, PROPOLIS, POLLEN, and BEESWAX, as well as various candle products made primarily from beeswax.

Over nearly two decades of development, we have accumulated profound industry experience and established a comprehensive quality management system. We consistently prioritize quality control from the source, ensuring that every product meets high standards. Guided by market demand, we continuously optimize our product portfolio and enhance our service levels, committed to providing customers with stable and high-quality product supply.

With reliable credibility and professional expertise, Hangzhou AADON TRADING has grown into a trusted long-term partner for clients both domestically and internationally. Moving forward, we will continue to adhere to the principle of excellence, working hand-in-hand with our customers to create mutual value.

Why Choose Us

Quality is not merely a checkpoint to pass, but a gene infused from the very beginning of a product's creation. It is a core commitment that runs through every step of production and every delivery. As a company deeply engaged in the international market, we understand that what you entrust to us is not just an order, but also your trust and the future of your market.
