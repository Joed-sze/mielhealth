Common Questions from Buyers

What is the minimum order quantity?

For most bee products, MOQ starts at 100kg. For beeswax candles, MOQ is 500 pieces. We also offer smaller trial/sample orders to help you evaluate quality before committing.

Do you provide product certifications?

Yes. Our partner factories hold HACCP, ISO 9001, KOSHER, HALAL, EU Organic, and USDA NOP certifications. COA and test reports are available for all products.

Can you arrange shipping to our country?

Yes. We handle sea freight for bulk orders and can arrange DHL/air freight for small or urgent orders. We provide full export documentation including health certificates and certificate of origin.
