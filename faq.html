Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our bee products and beeswax candles

Company & General

What is HANGZHOU AADON TRADING CO., LTD.?

HANGZHOU AADON TRADING CO., LTD. is a specialized international trading company based in Hangzhou, China. Since 2004, we have been exporting high-quality natural bee products and beeswax candles to clients worldwide. Our product range covers raw materials for the food, health supplement, cosmetics, and candle manufacturing industries.

Where are you located?

Our office is located at ROOM 6457, NO. 99, MEIYUAN SOUTH STREET, ZHUANGTANG STREET, XIHU DISTRICT, HANGZHOU, ZHEJIANG, CHINA. Hangzhou is conveniently located near Shanghai and Ningbo, two of China's most important international ports, ensuring efficient shipping logistics for our global customers.

How long have you been in business?

We have been in business since 2004, giving us over 20 years of experience in the bee products and beeswax candles export industry. Our long-standing relationships with suppliers and customers are a testament to our reliability and quality standards.

What markets do you serve?

We export our products to customers worldwide. Our clients include importers, retailers, and manufacturers in the food, health supplement, cosmetics, and candle industries across North America, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East.

Products

What bee products do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of bee products including: Natural Honey (raw and retail-packaged), Royal Jelly (fresh and freeze-dried powder), Propolis (refined extract, clean blocks, and propolis powder with various content levels up to 60%), Bee Pollen (for animal feeding, human consumption, and powder form), and Refined Beeswax (blocks and pellets). Visit our Bee Products page for more details.

What types of beeswax candles do you manufacture?

Our beeswax candle collection includes: Glass Candles, Handroll Candles, T-Light Candles, Braid Candles, Chanukah Candles, and Hanukkah Candles. All our candles are made from 100% natural beeswax, ensuring clean burning and long-lasting performance. View our full range on the Beeswax Candles page.

Are your products natural and pure?

Yes, all our bee products are 100% natural and pure. We work closely with our suppliers to ensure that our honey, royal jelly, propolis, and pollen are sourced from clean, unpolluted environments. Our beeswax candles are made from pure beeswax without any additives or synthetic materials.

Do you provide organic certified products?

Yes, our partner factories can provide organic certifications. We work with certified organic suppliers who hold EU Organic and NOP (USDA National Organic Program) certifications. Please contact us with your specific organic requirements.

Can I request custom packaging?

Yes, we offer custom packaging solutions for bulk orders. Whether you need private labeling, specific container sizes, or branded packaging, we can accommodate your requirements. Minimum order quantities apply for custom packaging. Please contact us to discuss your specific needs.

Quality & Certification

What quality control measures do you have?

We implement strict quality control at every stage: supplier qualification audits, raw material testing, production monitoring, and pre-shipment inspection. We collaborate with our suppliers as long-term strategic partners, utilizing tiered management and on-site technical support to ensure consistent quality.

What certifications do your products have?

Our partner factories hold the following certifications: HACCP (Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points), ISO 9001 (Quality Management System), KOSHER, HALAL, EU Organic Certification, and NOP (USDA National Organic Program) Certification. Specific certification availability may vary by product.

Do you provide product samples?

Yes, we provide product samples for serious buyers. Sample costs and shipping fees may apply depending on the product type and destination. Sample orders are a great way to evaluate our product quality before placing a bulk order. Contact us to request samples.

Ordering & Shipping

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Minimum order quantities vary by product. For bee products, MOQ starts from 100kg. For candles, our MOQ is 500 pieces/boxes. However, we are flexible and can discuss smaller quantities for trial orders.

What are your payment terms?

Our standard payment terms are T/T (Telegraphic Transfer) with 50% deposit and 50% balance before shipment. For long-term partners, we can discuss more flexible payment arrangements. We also accept L/C (Letter of Credit) for large orders.

How long is the delivery time?

Under normal circumstances, delivery takes 20-35 days after order confirmation. The exact timeline depends on the product type, order quantity, and destination port. We will provide a detailed delivery schedule when you place your order.

What shipping methods do you use?

We primarily use sea freight for bulk orders due to cost-effectiveness. For urgent or small orders, we can arrange air freight. We work with reliable freight forwarders to ensure safe and timely delivery to your designated port or warehouse.

Do you handle customs documentation?

Yes, we provide all necessary export documentation including commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, and health certificates where required. We ensure that all documentation complies with the import regulations of your country.
